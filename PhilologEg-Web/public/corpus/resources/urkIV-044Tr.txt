Transliteration and translation of Itef-ruri and his son Pa-heri,
following the transcription of Sethe (1927), number 44 (pp. 109-110).

The transliteration throughout follows Hannig (1995).

For published translations, see Sethe (1914), number 44 (pp. 54-55).
