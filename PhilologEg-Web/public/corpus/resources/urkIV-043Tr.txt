Transliteration and translation of inscription of Wadjemes in Western Thebes,
following the transcription of Sethe (1927), number 43 (p. 108).

The transliteration throughout follows Hannig (1995).

For published translations, see Sethe (1914), number 43 (p. 54).

der Pharaonen (2800-950 v.Chr.)</i>. Verlag Philipp von Zabern, 1995.

Hinrichs, Leipzig, 1914.
